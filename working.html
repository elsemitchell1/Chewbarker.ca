Police and Military

German Shepherds are often utilised in the police force and military. Their great sense of smell and hearing along with their high level of obedience make them the perfect candidates.

Protection

German Shepherds are naturally very protective of their owners and homes. They can have this natural instinct trained even further through the help of professional trainers also.

Herding

German Shepherds were originally bred for herding and guarding livestock. As a result they have very high prey drives and can also be very mouthy as pups. However, these natural instincts can be controlled with the right training. As a puppy Chewbarker loved chasing his cat siblings but training him to control his prey drive has really helped and now he gets along great with his siblings.

Family Dog

German Shepherds can potentially make great family dogs. However, they need plenty of training and guidance from a young age otherwise they can be extremely hard to control when they mature. They will also need constant supervision especially around younger children as they can sometimes play rough when they're excited.

About Chewbarker.ca

Chewbarker.ca is a collection of information specifically related to the German Shepherd breed. This site is a useful tool for anyone looking to learn more about this breed of dog or for current owners to stay up-to-date with the breed and connect with other GSD owners.
